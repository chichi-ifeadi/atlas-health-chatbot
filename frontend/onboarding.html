Welcome to Atlas, there.

Atlas is your personal wellness companion that helps you build healthier routines through guided support, daily check-ins, and simple progress insights.

Here's what Atlas can do for you.

01

Wellness guidance

Get personalized advice on sleep, stress, energy, nutrition, and daily routines.

02

Daily check-ins

Log how you feel each day and track patterns in your sleep, mood, and energy over time.

03

Trend insights

Your dashboard shows weekly patterns so you can understand what affects your wellbeing.

Before you begin

Atlas is a wellness support tool, not a medical service. It will not diagnose conditions, recommend medications, or replace professional healthcare.

For any medical concerns, always consult a qualified healthcare professional.

In a mental health crisis, Atlas will direct you to professional support resources including the 988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline.
