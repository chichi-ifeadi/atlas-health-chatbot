How it works

Three steps to a healthier you

1

Create your account

Sign up in seconds. Atlas keeps your data private and secure — your check-ins and conversations are yours alone.

2

Each morning or evening, spend 60 seconds logging how you feel. Atlas remembers your history and adjusts its guidance accordingly.

3

Chat, track, and improve

Ask Atlas for advice anytime. Review your trends on the dashboard. Watch your wellness scores climb as your habits improve.
